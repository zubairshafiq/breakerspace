Research Interests

My research involves measurement and modeling of emerging privacy and security threats in networked computer systems. I am particularly interested in using machine learning to counter online tracking and surveillance. Current research topics:

  • Privacy & Security (Web, Mobile, IoT, Social Media)

  • Networked Systems (Browsers, CDN, Cellular, Video)
